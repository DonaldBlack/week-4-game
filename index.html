Crystal Collector

Rules

You will start out with a randomly generated number.

There are 4 different colored crystals below. Each of those crystals has a different value. Every time you click one of them the value of the crystal will be added to "Your Number."

Match "Your Number" with the "Target Number" and you Win! Go over the Target Number and you lose

The value for each crystal is a mystery to you...... until you click them!
